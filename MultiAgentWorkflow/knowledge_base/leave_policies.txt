COLLEGE LEAVE POLICIES

1. Student Leave Allowances:
   - Students are allowed a maximum of 10 casual leaves per semester.
   - Any leave request must be submitted at least 24 hours in advance through the College Portal.
   - Medical leaves are capped at 15 days per semester and require a valid medical certificate from a registered practitioner.
   - Academic leaves for representing the college in technical, cultural, or sports events are permitted up to 10 days, subject to prior approval from the Dean of Student Affairs.
   - Taking unapproved leaves will result in marking the student absent and will count against their total attendance percentage.

2. Leave Approval Authority:
   - The designated Faculty Advisor has the authority to approve or reject leave requests of up to 3 days.
   - Any leave request exceeding 3 days must be approved by the Head of Department (HOD).
   - Medical leaves must be verified by the HOD and final approval is granted by the Principal.

3. Attendance Requirements:
   - A minimum of 75% attendance in each registered course is mandatory to be eligible to write the end-semester examinations.
   - A condonation of up to 10% (making it 65% minimum) may be granted on medical grounds or sports representation, subject to HOD recommendation and payment of a condonation fee.
   - Students with attendance below 65% in any course will be debarred from the examination for that course and must register for it again in subsequent semesters.
